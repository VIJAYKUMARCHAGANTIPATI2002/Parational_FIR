About Us

A novel approach of case filing, utilising online platform to streamline benefits access and replace the need for in-person visits in partially to police stations. The traditional process of reporting incidents seeking assistance and accessing benefits often involves time consuming and resource-intensive interactions at physical police stations.



The proposed online case filing system enables individuals to report incidents,file complaints and request various benefits secure online platform.Through user- friendly interface individuals can provide detailed information about incident attach relevant documents or evidence,and select the specific benefits they require.The system also ensures the privacy and confidentiality the personal data through robust to security measures and data protection protocols.
